My Favorite Place

This is a waterfall in the trails of White Park, Morgantown WV. Its a beautiful place that starts at an overhanging cave opening, where the waterfall crashes down onto a trail of slate rock. From there it pools down into a little pond thats safe for swimming and wading. It then falls again from an overhang into a large stream eventually leading to the river. Its a beautiful place to have a picnic, swim with friends and family, and hike the path. It's my favorite because of the view and secluded space it gives, it's very peacefull for meditation.
